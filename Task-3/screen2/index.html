RED CARD 2018

China Digital Football Awards

The Red Card Awards is an annual report that celebrates the best and worst of football digital marketing in China. It is based on a comprehensive analysis of the digital presence of football clubs in China, including their websites, social media accounts, and online campaigns.

This report aims to educate teams and organisations on how to improve their China digital strategy and what works online in China.
